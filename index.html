A letter to my lapuk

My dearest baby, You once asked me the reasons why I love you. I remember struggling to answer, not because there were none, but because my words were too small to hold them. How can I explain a feeling that simply exists, like the way breathing exists? I love you for all the reasons I know, and even for the ones I cannot name. I love the way you smile, how your eyes squint like your ancestors when you do. It always makes me laugh. I love how you make me feel loved and special, the way you speak to me so gently as if I am something so fragile and the way your soft voice soothes me. It may sound cliché, but it truly is music to my ears, especially when you call me “baby.” How can I not love you when we share the same humor? There is never a day we do not laugh like two carefree souls, as if the world forgets it's weight for just a moment. So how much do I really love you? I love you with all my hypothalamus. because if this is all biology, Why does it feel like love is rewriting my anatomy? But you know what? fuck science… I love you. So yes, this is my answer. Yes, I will be your girlfriend. Yes, I will walk beside you in this life. Because when I imagine the years ahead, I cannot see anyone else there. Only you. ❤️
